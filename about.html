Our History

Founded in 2010, TechCon began as a small gathering of 200 tech enthusiasts in Silicon Valley. Over the years, we've grown into one of the world's premier technology conferences, hosting over 10,000 attendees annually.

Our Mission

To bridge the gap between emerging technologies and practical implementation by fostering collaboration among developers, entrepreneurs, and industry leaders.
